Privacy Policy

By using HunterSuite, you consent to the collection and use of your data as we have outlined in this policy and to our Agreement. CySec NG may decide to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post those changes on HunterSuite website, so that you are always aware of the data we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it. HunterSuite notes and saves information such as your e-mail, browser type, browser language, and IP address. We do not collect credit cards information.
